Projects

Automatic Plant Watering System

The Automatic Plant Watering System is an Arduino-based smart irrigation project designed to water plantsautomatically whenever the soil becomes dry. It helps reduce water wastage and ensures that plants receive the right amount of water without manual effort.

This project uses a soil moisture sensor to detect the moisture level in the soil. When the soil is dry, the sensor sends a signal to the Arduino, which activates a relay module to switch on the water pump.Once the soil reaches the required moisture level, the pump automatically turns off.

Through this project, I gained practical experience in Arduino programming, sensor interfacing, relay control, hardware connections, and basic embedded systems. It also improved my problem-solving and teamwork skills while developing a real-world solution.
